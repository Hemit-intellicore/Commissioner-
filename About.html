About Hon. Anna Benjamin Itodo

A visionary leader driving women empowerment and social development in Benue State.

Who Is Anna Benjamin Itodo?

Hon. Anna Benjamin Itodo serves as the Commissioner for Women Affairs and Social Development, Benue State. Known for her unwavering dedication to uplifting the status of women and children, she is a beacon of hope and progress. Her innovative programs have transformed countless lives, fostering economic empowerment, education, and social inclusion.

With over a decade of experience in public service, Anna combines passion with expertise to deliver impactful solutions to societal challenges. Her work reflects her commitment to sustainable development and creating opportunities for all, particularly the most vulnerable in society.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission

To champion the rights and opportunities for women and children, fostering their empowerment through strategic programs and policies. We aim to create an inclusive society where everyone thrives, regardless of gender or background.

Vision

To build a society where every woman and child has the opportunity to achieve their potential, contributing meaningfully to the development of Benue State and Nigeria at large.
