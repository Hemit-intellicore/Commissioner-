Meet Hon. Anna Benjamin Itodo

Hon. Anna Benjamin Itodo has dedicated her life to advancing women's rights and promoting social development. Her innovative programs and initiatives continue to impact thousands of lives across Benue State and beyond.

  • Leader in child protection programs.
